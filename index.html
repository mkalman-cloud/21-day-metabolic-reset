21 Day Metabolic Reset

Supporting body fat loss, muscle retention, gut health, and nervous system regulation

This program integrates alkaline nutrition, strength training, hydration, and gentle psychoeducation to support energy, mood, and long term wellbeing.

Use this guide at your own pace. Progress comes from consistency, not perfection.

Important Disclaimer

This program is provided for educational and informational purposes only. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any medical condition.

Always consult with a qualified health professional before making significant changes to your diet, exercise routine, or supplementation, especially if you have a medical condition, are pregnant, or are taking medication.

Participation is voluntary. Listen to your body and modify or stop any activity that does not feel safe or appropriate for you.

Before You Start Checklist

  • Clear your kitchen of foods that feel inflammatory or emotionally triggering
  • Purchase groceries from the shopping list
  • Prepare your daily Herbalife hydration mix
  • Decide which training days you may choose to do, without pressure
  • Commit to self respect rather than perfection
  • Remind yourself this is a reset, not a test
This program supports your body, gut, brain, and nervous system. You are allowed to go slowly. You are allowed to rest. You are allowed to adapt.

If You Miss a Day

Nothing is broken. Your nervous system learns through repetition, not perfection. Simply continue with the next day. Consistency over time creates safety and results.
